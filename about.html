About US

The Carl Creations Company, commonly shortened to CC, is a Zimbabwean multinational information technology company headquartered in Harare the capital of Zimbabwe. It develops and provides a wide variety of hardware components, as well as software and related services to consumers, small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs) and large enterprises, including customers in the government, health and education sectors. The company was founded by a student at Harare Institute of Technology in Harare, by Carl Murimba in 2021, and initially produced a line of electronic test and measurement equipment.
